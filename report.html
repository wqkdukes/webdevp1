Project Report

  1. I made this website as a basic proof of concept for my stepdad. He's a freelance arist, and does a lot of his work online so having his own website would be great.
  2. All images are originally from David Kennedy and all of the HTML/CSS was developed by me.
  3. There are five pages the home/landing page, an about page, a gallery, a page giving information about pricing, and a page to contact David through a form.

  6. I use media queries to stack things on top of eachother when the screen width becomes too narrow.

  8. All images have alt tags that describe that images purpose.
  9. Each page has a camera as its favicon.
  10. All images and css files are placed into their own folders.
  11. No libraries or frameworks used.
  12. All pages were validated after I ran them through the validator and fixed them up.
  13. I think the layout of my site was best served by keeping my elements centered. Though when I had lines of text I would keep it left-aligned.
  14. Spacing and font sizing is consistent across pages.
  15. All of the different pages have them same font while the header is unique.
  16. There is a form for contacting David on the contact page.
